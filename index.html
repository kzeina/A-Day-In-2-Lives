Jaipur is known as the Pink City, is famous for its beautiful palaces, colorful streets, and traditional jewellery. Growing up in Jaipur has taught me to notice beauty in the smallest things, from the blue pottery to the peacocks I see on the side of the road sometimes, and to appreciate how much there is to experience beyond the obvious. I love Jaipur because it feels like a perfect mix of rich history and a place I can always call home.
